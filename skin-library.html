The craft

Made in
Hội An.

01
Weave

Raw Vietnamese silk, handwoven on traditional looms in the Ma Chau village — 17 generations of weaving knowledge in every thread.

02
Cut

Each skin is cut to the exact dimensions of its chassis size — seam positions mapped to align with the steel rib positions precisely.

03
Treat

Fire retardancy treatment applied to meet AS/NZS 1530.2 — the Australian standard for interior textile flame spread. Zero compromise on safety.

04
Finish

Elasticated hem fitted, hub-lock collar sewn, final quality inspection. Each skin is packaged individually — ready to ship to any address in Australia.
