02 — The skin

Vietnamese silk.
180–250gsm.

Each skin is cut and sewn in Hội An to match the chassis rib positions precisely. Seams disappear when lit. The weave irregularities that look like imperfection are the source of the warm, diffused glow.

How the
system works.

The bayonet-lock hub is the heart of the KYUNO system. Three steps. No tools. No compromise on the structural integrity of the chassis.

01
Fit the skin

Align the silk skin's elasticated hem to the upper hub collar. The seams are designed to disappear against the steel ribs when lit.

02
Twist to lock

Rotate the lower hub ring 30° clockwise. Four bayonet lugs engage. A subtle click confirms the skin is secured.

03
Live with it

The chassis handles all structural load. The skin is pure surface — warm amber light filters through, revealing the weave in full.
